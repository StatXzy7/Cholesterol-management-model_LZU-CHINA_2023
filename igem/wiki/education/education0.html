Education


Education, the primary tool to elevate human quality and promote human advancement. Knowledge, akin to science, loses its significance if confined to only a few hands. A successful research project should also be dedicated to disseminating its scientific insights. Humanity can only progress when an increasing number of people gain more knowledge. Education is not one-dimensional. As said in "The Poetics," "to educate through entertainment." We believe that the pinnacle of education is to provide both knowledge and joy to the learners. Mr. Lei Feng once said, "One's life is finite, but serving the people is infinite. I want to devote my limited life to the infinite service of the people." We too dedicate ourselves in the limited time we have. Being part of the iGEM project and researching such valuable topics is our greatest honor. Hence, we strive to invest our limited time in as many diverse and enriching education activities as we can.

We categorize our education initiatives into online and offline approaches.

Online Education


We used PVC board and printed out the plasmids and gene fragments. Then we let the participants receive the corresponding gene fragment at each locus after a correct answer based on the plasmid randomly received at the beginning, finally they followed the circular route back to starting point. In the final part of our game, participants need to splice gene fragments on the plasmid in the right order, making the plasmid can achieve the therapeutic effect. In the end, according to the time taken by each group of participants to complete the entire activity as the basis of the ranking, the top three will be awarded with small prizes related to biology.

To our surprise, after the announcement of the activity, many students actively signed up, which we had not expected. The number of teams or individuals who wanted to participate was far more than the 20 groups or persons we had expected.


As our first education event with such a large number of participants, it reminds us that we should make a good prediction of the activity volume in the future activities, something like the pre-survey questionnaire will be beneficial, so that the public who are interested in synthetic biology can participate in our activities as much as possible.

Intellectual Disabled People


This education event was jointly organized by LZU-CHINA and SCUT-China two iGEM teams, and the event was held at the Tianhe Work Therapy Station in Guangzhou City, Guangdong Province. The activities were aimed at people receiving treatment at Work Therapy Stations. The "Work Therapy Station" is a living place that provides work and treatment for intellectual disabled people, the disabled and the mentally ill (who have generally recovered) in the community. We chose them as our educational group because they usually lack communication with the outside world, which they have limited access to synthetic biology. We also believe that one of the purposes of synthetic biology is to benefit special groups like them, so that barriers between people can be removed and the world can become a more equitable and free place.

The purpose of the work therapy activities is to make the workers realize their own value, and at the same time, let all the intellectually handicapped people have a place to communicate and learn. This activity aims to build a communication bridge between our team and vulnerable groups in society; we also hope to enrich the amateur life of the intellectually handicapped people by making crafts related to synthetic biology. Our team have held several similar events before this, covering multiple groups including children, college students and more. Our purpose is also to make synthetic biology known and accepted by more people, so as to create favorable conditions for the development of synthetic biology in China.

After consultation, our two teams have formulated a new way to let them appreciate the beauty of biology. Our team prepared the basic materials included butterfly specimens, fake flowers, watercolor pens, glue and other tools. One of the butterfly specimens was collected during our scientific research in the Qilian Mountains, with the permission of the local environmental protection bureau, we want people to feel the beauty of nature and the wonders of animals. SCUT-China bring the functional silk developed, which can eliminate the public's misunderstanding and resistance to genetic modification. We hope they will be able to use these tools to stitch and decorate on paper and finally assemble their favorite artwork. In order to make it easier for them to take home the artwork they have created, we have also prepared a picture frame so that they can pack the finished product in the frame and take it away.

At 2:30 pm 30/08/2022, our activity was officially launched at Tianhe Work Therapy Station. The event consisted of 10 participators and 5 team members from LZU-CHINA and SCUT-China. Primary School Science and Technology Week


Due to the pressure to enter higher education, primary school students in China are basically receiving exam-oriented education and have little exposure to synthetic biology. When we found out about this situation, we believed that it is necessary to conduct educational activities for elementary school students. Therefore, we participated in their Technology and Science Week of Lanzhou University Affiliated Primary School.

First, we started with a simple question: "If we eat genetically modified food, will we be genetically modified? Why?" The children were more passionate than we could have imagined, and they enthusiastically expressed their views. We then talked about genetically modified foods and asked the children what they thought about genetically modified foods. Some of them felt that genetic modification would lead to a better variety of food, while others felt that genetically modified food is harmful to health. This was used to explain to the children the function of genes in living organisms and the basic structure of DNA. Through these two questions, we gave the children an initial understanding of genes.

photo of our lecture start

The second part of the science activity was to introduce the children to genetic engineering and its application in their lives. We used the manufacturing process of some vaccines as an example to illustrate to the children that the use of genetic engineering can benefit mankind. Then we showed promotion video of ourselves and NEU_CHINA to introduce our project to the children, who showed a very strong interest. Of course, in order to facilitate their understanding, we replaced some of the specialized vocabulary in the promotion video and explained it to them in more interesting language. Both we and NEU_CHINA are genetically modifying microbes from different angles and hope to inspire them from multiple perspectives.

elementary school students were watching our team's science video
elementary school students were watching NEU_CHINA's science video

After watching the video, we made a brief summary of today's content. Subsequently, the intense and exciting question answering session was conducted, and students who answered the questions correctly first will receive LZU-CHINA and NEU-CHINA's IP products.

students were very active in answering questions

At the end of the event, we gave a small gift to each student who participated in the lecture, and also distributed a large doll as a reward to the students who answered the most questions. In the end, everyone happily took a big group photo with the prizes to end the day's activities.

children held up their prizes and took a group photo with us

After the event, we distributed a questionnaire to check the effectiveness of the event. Through everyone's feedback, the students had the highest evaluation of the preemptive answer session, and the evaluation of the classroom explanation and video introduction was also relatively good.

children held up their prizes and took a group photo with us

We asked them how well they understood the content and how much you would rate yourself. Most of them gave high points The final average score of 89.94 points proved that the students were generally very satisfied with their performance in this event and proved that we successfully stimulated their enthusiasm for the life sciences field.

Then we asked them if they had been exposed to science popularization activities on similar topics before and whether they had previously learned about the field of biology. The final results show that more than 80% of the students have been in contact with popular science lectures on related topics and have a certain understanding of the direction of biology. This is obviously one of the reasons why the response to this event has exceeded expectations.

In the end, the students generally felt that the activity was very interesting and gained a lot from it. When asked what else they thought, more than half of the students hoped that such activities would be carried out more to enrich their knowledge. That's what education is all about. Through education, we promote the knowledge of genetically modified organisms and synthetic biology, so that they can be understood by everyone for later promotion. Children as the future of the country, to promote to them, will plant the seeds of science in their hearts, so that they can lay a foundation for the development of these aspects in the future.

Biological Band Performance


We have thought of many ways to combine popular elements with synthetic biology in order to better promote synthetic biology among teenagers. We have worked with SCUT-China in Guangzhou on an event that combined arts and crafts with biology for a special group of people, and the results were so good that we tried to think of other ways of combining arts with synthetic biology in order to promote it more smoothly among teenagers.

Happily, a number of our team members are very interested in music and several of us play instruments or sing, and we hit it off and decided to combine popular music with synthetic biology. ICII : A Large Collaborative Education Campaign in China


Bringing synthetic biology to the world is one of the core contents of iGEM. As a member of iGEM family, it is our duty to promote synthetic biology to the world. However, academic preaching activities often refer to small-scale professionals, and we hope that synthetic biology can be more easily understood by more people. Therefore, we participated in the national publicity campaign ICII, and carried out popular science education activities with other teams in China for the general population without professional knowledge background.

posters of ICII

In August 2022, we joined the ICII family and worked with many teams to prepare the iGEM online science promotion campaign. ICII, whose full name is 'Into CHINA, Into iGEM', is a large-scale cooperative event promoting synthetic biology and popular science education. This year 's ICII was hosted by NAU-CHINA, and a total of 12 iGEM teams from different circuits across the country were invited to participate. LZU-CHINA was one of them. Unfortunately, due to the impact of the novel coronavirus epidemic, we had to choose to carry out it in the form of live webcasts and cancel offline tours.

After discussion within the team, we believe that it is unrealistic to let the public fully understand the content of synthetic biology through a live science broadcast. Therefore, we shifted the focus of our work from 'depth' to 'breadth', hoping to establish the mass consciousness related to synthetic biology as much as possible through this activity. At the same time, we also hope that this 'consciousness' can help the masses in some cases to make different, more correct decisions. Finally, we finalized the theme 'the Principle and Development of Tumor-Targeted Bacterial Therapy '. This is because although the treatment methods based on synthetic biology are developing rapidly, in fact, such treatment methods are often not accepted by the public, so it is necessary to popularize them.

broadcasting live on the Bilibili platform

During our broadcasting live lecture, we mainly introduce the origin and development of bacterial therapy, also introduce its principle in a straightforward way. At the same time, we have carried out the current situation of tumor treatment, tumor treatment methods and the characteristics of bacterial therapy. The purpose is to tell the public the advantages of bacterial therapy and improve the public acceptance. Finally, our live broadcast reached more than 100 people's views. After the end of the live broadcast, there have been audience in the background to ask us about the content, which is very gratifying. In addition, iGEM team BIT-CHINA took note of us through this live broadcast and discussed the use of related genetic parts with us. We are pleased to take this opportunity to help other iGEM teams.

In addition, in order to make iGEM activities more close to the local, LZU-CHINA combines the urban culture of Lanzhou city to promote iGEM and Lanzhou university, showing the world China's rich and colorful traditional culture, so that the world knows more about China.

Artists from our team also contributed artworks.

Gut Alliance


We were fortunate to meet a number of teams working on gut microbiology at the Northwest meeting. Together we formed the Gut Alliance to make our voices heard. We invited experts and health care professionals in gut microbiology to give presentations to make the public understand gut microbiology. We actively communicated with each other in the "Gut Alliance" group chat to discuss the format of the event. After deciding on the format of the event, each team prepared a topic for popularization.

On August 14, our activity officially started with a video of blessing from President Canhui Lan, president of the Institute of Enthusiastic Biotechnology and Executive Chairman of the China Intestinal Conference. After that we were honored to invite Prof. Daixu Wei to give us a lecture entitled "Intestine and Tracts". Finally, Nurse Director Xiaojuan Dong from the Fifth People's Hospital of Shaanxi Province gave us a lecture entitled "Those things about upper gastrointestinal hemorrhage". Then came the science of each team.

NWU-CHINA-A took us to unravel the mystery of H. pylori, WS_HZBIOX introduces the relationship between sugar substitute and intestinal flora, LZU-CHINA introduces the occurrence and detection of colorectal cancer, NEU-CHINA introduces the damage of sugar-free drinks on intestinal health flora and the role of prebiotics and probiotics on intestinal health, LZU-HS-PRO-A introduces the principle of gut-brain axis, HS-China's iGEMer introduced lactose intolerance and the treatment of lactose intolerance. The event was attended by nearly 100 people on the day, which caused a strong response.

We condensed the event into a "Gut Alliance Brochure" and distributed it to everyone online to make the impact of our program even more profound.

Mid-Autumn Lantern


The fluorescent protein was expressed by E. coli to make it fluorescence, and the luminescent bacteria was wrapped in hydrogel, which recipe provided by NEU_CHINA. The hydrogel protects the strain well from being taken out of the lab, so we started with a tour of the fluorescent bacteria encased in the hydrogel to give people an initial insight into E. coli and transgenics. We had everyone properly disinfected during the entire visit in and out of the lab, and we were the only ones who had access to the genetically modified fluorescent bacteria throughout. So the whole activity does not bring microorganisms out of the laboratory.

the fluorescent bacteria encased in the hydrogel we showed to audience

Just in time for the Mid-Autumn Festival, lanterns are a traditional custom for the Chinese Mid-Autumn Festival. We use the lantern to symbolize the E. coli carrier. We made lanterns that glow with light color same as the protein, GFP and RFP, which the whole lantern is a symbol of transgenic E. coli. We bought some simple materials and together we made the outside of the lantern.

the process we made lanterns

We used the lanterns as a festive atmosphere to celebrate the Mid-Autumn Festival with our school teachers and students, as well as with the staff who work for us, and to promote and educate people about the principles of making fluorescent lanterns. Finally, we also gave these lanterns as holiday gifts to the cafeteria staff to thank them for their thoughtful and elaborate service to the students and teachers.
